Sn@il's Tales

A blog about development

The posts in this blog examine the cultural and technical landscape of software, web, and app development. The earliest posts (August-September) discuss basic coding principles and tech competencies (e.g., fundamentals of Git and Github). New coders will find this blog most helpful, though seasoned programmers will hopefully find some happy amusement in these playful musings. Perhaps most importantly, this blog traces the very linear, but still exciting, exhausting, and sometimes seemingly impossible journey of a junior programmer, learning about life, love, and code at Dev Bootcamp.
