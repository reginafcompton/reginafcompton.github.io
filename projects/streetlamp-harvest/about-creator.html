Streetlamp Harvest

Regina Compton

Creator of Streetlamp Harvest

Regina does not have a front-yard, backyard, or side-yard, nor a porch or fire escape upon which to grow herbs, flowers, and jalapeno peppers. Instead, she resides in a 180-square-foot apartment in the heart of Chicago, where she can appreciate, envy, and despise the miniature gardens that line the streets of her neighborhood.

Currently, Regina is learning about computer languages and the behind-the-scenes stories of the Internet. You can follow her journey into the twenty-first-century on her personal website, Before the Sn@il. You can also send her an email (reginafcompton@gmail.com), or follow her on Twitter, Github, and LinkedIn.
